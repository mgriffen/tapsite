Capabilities

What it does

Three modes of intelligence. One unified toolkit.

Ex

Extract

Pull design systems, color palettes, font stacks, spacing scales, components, and breakpoints from any live website. Structured JSON, every time.

Au

Audit

Run accessibility scores, contrast ratio checks, performance timing analysis, dark mode detection, and stack fingerprinting without leaving your agent.

Di

Compare

Diff pages across sites or across time. Track design drift, detect regressions, and build temporal intelligence with snapshot-based analysis.

Workflow

Three steps to intelligence

No browser automation scripts. No fragile selectors. Just ask.

1

Connect

Add tapsite as an MCP server to your AI agent. Works with Claude, Cursor, Windsurf, and any MCP-compatible client.

2

Navigate

Browse to any URL, including sites behind login walls. Manual login with MFA support keeps credentials off the wire.

3

Extract

Run any of 43 specialized tools. Get structured JSON intelligence on design systems, content, accessibility, performance, and more.
